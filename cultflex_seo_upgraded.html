HOW TO MEASURE YOURSELF
CHEST: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
SHOULDER: Measure from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across your back.
LENGTH: Measure from the top of the shoulder to the hem at the bottom.
SLEEVE: From shoulder tip to wrist, arm slightly bent.

Tip: Wear a well-fitting shirt while measuring for the most accurate results.
